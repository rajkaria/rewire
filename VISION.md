# Rewire — Vision

## The Problem

Humanity has accumulated thousands of years of wisdom about how to live well. Ancient scriptures, philosophy, modern psychology — the knowledge exists. And yet:

- People read self-help books, feel inspired for a week, then revert to old patterns.
- The gap between knowing and doing is where most personal growth dies.
- Books can't follow up. Books can't ask "how did that go?" Books can't adapt to your specific situation.

## The Insight

AI agents can do what books cannot. They remember context across sessions. They ask targeted questions. They follow up on commitments. They adapt their approach when something isn't working. They never judge, never tire, and are available at 3am when you can't sleep because of anxiety.

But out of the box, AI agents are generic conversationalists. They need structured coaching protocols to coach effectively — the same way a therapist needs training, not just intelligence.

## What Rewire Does

Rewire provides those protocols. Each skill encodes a coaching methodology — not book content, but the *practice* of coaching someone through behavior change using that methodology.

The skill format is:
- **SKILL.md** — The coaching protocol: how to assess, teach, practice, and track
- **Lessons** — Teaching playbooks for the agent (what concepts to convey, what questions to ask)
- **Exercises** — Interactive practices the agent guides the user through
- **Tracking** — Check-in protocols and progress milestones

## The Skill Spectrum

Rewire deliberately spans three categories:

**Ancient Wisdom** — The Bhagavad Gita, Meditations, Tao Te Ching, Dhammapada. These texts have coached humans for millennia. Encoding them as AI skills makes timeless wisdom accessible in a modern, interactive format.

**Modern Psychology** — Atomic Habits, CBT, NVC, logotherapy. Evidence-based approaches to behavior change, emotional regulation, and communication.

**Applied Life Domains** — Financial psychology, deep work, relationships, mindset. Practical domains where coaching makes an immediate difference.

This mix is deliberate. Rewire is not just "self-help books as AI prompts." It's a bridge between thousand-year-old wisdom and AI-native coaching.

## Composability

Skills are designed to work together. When a user working on habits reveals deeper anxiety, the agent can draw from CBT techniques. When someone practicing Stoic philosophy needs actionable systems, it can bridge to behavior design.

Pre-designed compound protocols combine skills for common journeys:
- **Founder's Journey:** karma-yoga + atomic-habits + deep-work + money-mind
- **Anxiety Management:** feeling-good + mindful-path + stoic-meditations
- **Relationship Repair:** attachment-styles + nonviolent-communication + way-of-tao

## Quality Philosophy

**Coach, don't summarize.** A skill that merely presents information is not coaching. Coaching means assessment, adaptation, accountability, and follow-up.

**The craft is in the questions.** The difference between a mediocre coach and a great one is the quality of questions they ask. Skills encode specific, powerful questions — not generic prompts.

**Encode failure modes.** Knowing how people typically misapply a methodology is as valuable as the methodology itself. Every skill includes common pitfalls and how to redirect.

**Respect the source.** Skills encode coaching methodologies in original words. No book quotes, no passage paraphrasing. A skill should work as standalone coaching even if the user has never read the source material.

## The Roadmap

**Phase 1 — Foundation:** 12 launch skills across all three tiers. Three flagships at full depth, nine at launchable depth. Open-source on GitHub.

**Phase 2 — Community:** Grow to 20+ skills through contributions. Establish practitioner badges, playtesting protocols, and therapist review pipeline.

**Phase 3 — Intelligence:** Build a meta-skill that assesses users and routes them to the right skills. Create a Wisdom Graph mapping connections across all skills.

**Phase 4 — Ecosystem:** AI-assisted skill creation from books. Author partnerships. Research into what effective AI coaching looks like at scale.

## The Ultimate Goal

Make AI better at caring. Not in a shallow, sycophantic way — in the deep, structured, accountability-driven way that changes lives. The kind of caring that asks hard questions, remembers what you said you'd do, and doesn't let you off the hook.

The wisdom already exists. The technology is ready. Rewire connects them.
