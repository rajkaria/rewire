# Character Strengths Coaching

> Discover what is already strong in you — and build your life around it.

## What This Skill Does

Coaches users through identifying their signature character strengths and learning to deploy them deliberately in daily life. Based on the VIA Classification of Character Strengths, which identifies 24 universal character qualities that appear across cultures and throughout history. This is not about fixing weaknesses — it is about finding what already works and doing more of it.

## Source

VIA Character Strengths framework, developed by Martin Seligman and Christopher Peterson as part of positive psychology research. The classification of 24 strengths organized under six virtues (wisdom, courage, humanity, justice, temperance, transcendence) represents the most comprehensive scientific effort to catalog human goodness.

## What You Will Work On

- Identifying your top 5-7 signature strengths through self-observation and guided assessment
- Understanding the difference between strengths and skills — and why it matters
- Recognizing when strengths are overused (becoming liabilities) or underused (leaving you drained)
- Deliberately deploying strengths in work, relationships, and daily routines
- Spotting strengths in others to improve your relationships and collaboration

## Structure

- **4 lessons** covering strength discovery, strengths in action, strengths at work, and strengths in relationships
- **3 exercises** including a guided self-assessment, a week-long daily strength challenge, and a deployment planning session
- **Weekly check-ins** with progress tracking
- **4 milestones** from awareness through mastery
