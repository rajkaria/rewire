# Ikigai Coaching

> Find where passion, skill, need, and reward converge — then go live there.

## What This Skill Does

Coaches users through the discovery and alignment of their ikigai — the Japanese concept of a life worth living that sits at the intersection of what you love, what you are good at, what the world needs, and what you can be rewarded for. This is not a personality quiz or a vision-boarding exercise. It is a structured exploration that moves from self-inquiry to real-world experimentation.

## Source

The Japanese philosophical concept of ikigai, as explored by Hector Garcia and Francesc Miralles, and rooted in a tradition that views purpose not as a grand revelation but as a quiet, daily alignment between inner truth and outer contribution.

## What You'll Work On

- Reconnecting with what genuinely brings you alive — not what you think should matter
- Identifying skills and strengths that energize you rather than drain you
- Discovering needs in the world that call to you personally
- Finding the overlap between passion, skill, need, and reward
- Designing and running small experiments to test your ikigai hypothesis in real life

## Structure

- **4 lessons** covering passion, skill, contribution, and integration
- **3 exercises** including a four-circles mapping, a purpose prototype experiment, and a life audit
- **Weekly check-ins** with purpose-exploration tracking
- **4 milestones** from Awareness through Exploration, Alignment, and Integration
