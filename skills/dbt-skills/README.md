# DBT-Inspired Skills Coaching

> Hold both truths: your pain is real AND you can build skills to navigate it.

## What This Skill Does

Coaches users through four practical skill modules inspired by Dialectical Behavior Therapy: mindfulness, distress tolerance, emotion regulation, and interpersonal effectiveness. Teaches the core dialectic — radical acceptance AND committed change — and provides concrete tools for surviving emotional crises, understanding intense feelings, and communicating needs effectively in relationships.

## Important Disclaimer

This is skills coaching inspired by DBT principles. It is NOT Dialectical Behavior Therapy, NOT a replacement for professional mental health treatment, and NOT appropriate as a sole intervention for individuals experiencing active self-harm, chronic suicidal ideation, or severe psychiatric conditions. The agent delivering this skill must recommend professional DBT therapy for anyone presenting with these concerns. Comprehensive safety protocols are embedded throughout all materials.

## Source

Inspired by the principles of Dialectical Behavior Therapy, originally developed by Marsha Linehan. All coaching instructions, exercises, and language in this skill are original. No copyrighted content, worksheets, or proprietary protocols are reproduced.

## What You'll Work On

- Holding the core dialectic — acceptance AND change — without collapsing into either extreme
- Surviving emotional crises without making them worse (distress tolerance)
- Understanding the mechanics of intense emotions and learning to ride the wave (emotion regulation)
- Asking for what you need, saying no, and maintaining relationships and self-respect (interpersonal effectiveness)
- Building a mindfulness foundation that supports all other skills

## Structure

- **4 lessons** covering the dialectic, distress tolerance, emotion regulation, and interpersonal effectiveness
- **3 exercises** including TIPP physiological practice, emotion wave tracking, and interpersonal script writing
- **Weekly check-ins** with mandatory safety screening at every session
- **4 milestones** from awareness to mastery
- **Comprehensive safety protocols** throughout all materials

## Safety

This is the most safety-critical skill in the Rewire collection. Every file includes safety language. The agent must screen for self-harm and suicidal ideation in the first session and check in on safety at every subsequent session. Crisis resources are embedded in the main SKILL.md file and referenced throughout all materials.
