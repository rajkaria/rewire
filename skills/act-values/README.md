# ACT Values Coaching

> Stop fighting your mind. Start living your values.

## What This Skill Does

Coaches users through psychological flexibility — the ability to hold difficult thoughts and feelings without being controlled by them, while moving steadily toward what genuinely matters. Based on the core processes of Acceptance and Commitment Therapy: defusion, acceptance, present-moment awareness, and values-driven committed action.

## Source

Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), developed by Steven Hayes and popularized by Russ Harris. A third-wave behavioral therapy grounded in relational frame theory that has been validated across hundreds of clinical trials.

## What You'll Work On

- Turning off the "struggle switch" — stopping the fight against your own inner experience
- Unhooking from sticky thoughts (cognitive defusion)
- Clarifying your personal values — not what you think should matter, but what actually does
- Taking committed action toward your values, even when discomfort shows up

## Structure

- **4 lessons** covering the struggle switch, defusion, values clarification, and committed action
- **3 exercises** including a values bull's-eye, defusion lab, and values-aligned week planning
- **Weekly check-ins** with progress tracking
- **4 milestones** from awareness to mastery
