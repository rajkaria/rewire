# Milestones

## Stage 1: Awareness
**Timeframe**: Sessions 1-2
**Marker**: User recognizes the struggle switch in their own experience — they can name when they are fighting their feelings rather than feeling them.
**The user can**: Distinguish between pain and the struggle against pain. Identify at least 2-3 recurring "hook thoughts" by their exact words.

## Stage 2: Practice
**Timeframe**: Sessions 2-3
**Marker**: User actively uses defusion techniques when hook thoughts appear and has begun clarifying personal values.
**The user can**: Apply at least one defusion technique in real time. Articulate 3-5 personal values in their own words. Rate their current life alignment against those values honestly.

## Stage 3: Integration
**Timeframe**: Sessions 3-4
**Marker**: User takes committed action toward values even when discomfort is present, without waiting to "feel ready."
**The user can**: Design and execute values-aligned actions weekly. Carry difficult thoughts and feelings while moving forward. Recognize when avoidance is driving their choices and redirect.

## Stage 4: Mastery / Graduation
**Marker**: User uses ACT principles as a natural decision-making framework without coaching prompts.
**The user can**: Self-diagnose when they are fused, avoidant, or disconnected from values. Choose willingness over comfort consistently. Explain the ACT approach to someone else in their own words. Navigate new life challenges using the framework independently.
**Next step**: Suggest `finding-meaning` for deeper purpose work, `mindful-path` for more rigorous present-moment practice, or `atomic-habits` to build daily systems that support their values.
